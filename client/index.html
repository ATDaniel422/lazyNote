How to use lazyNote

Step 1: Record

The first step is record your audio. Make sure you have a stable internet connection and are withing hearing distance of the speaker. Press the Record button when you're ready.

Step 2: Be Patient

As soon as you press record, the process of turning speach into a readable document begins. Please don't close your computer until you are finished recording, otherwise it may disrupt your data.

Step 3: Submit Your Email

Once you are finished recording, hit the Stop button to finish the recording. Enter your email address and hit the Submit button. Don't worry, we won't spam you mailbox. lazyNote just needs to know where to send the text file once it's ready.

Look for an Email from Us

Once our system is finished processing the data, we will email you a link so that you can download your text file wherever it's most convenient for you. Thanks for using lazyNote!
